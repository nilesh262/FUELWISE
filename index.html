The Problem

Fuel prices in India are a maze.
No one helps you navigate it.

Fuel is not under GST. Each state sets its own VAT — creating a price spread of up to ₹27 per litre. Yet no single platform compares prices across all oil companies for your specific route.

How It Works

Three inputs. One optimal plan.

Enter your vehicle, your route, and your current fuel level. FuelWise does the rest.

1 🚗

Set your vehicle

Select your vehicle type, fuel preference, mileage, and tank capacity for personalized calculations.

2 🗺️

Enter your route

Type your starting point and destination. We map every fuel station along your path with real-time prices.

3 💰

Get the cheapest plan

Our algorithm calculates where to stop, at which station, and how many litres to fill — minimizing your total fuel cost.

Core Features

Built for real savings,
not just price listings.

FuelWise goes beyond showing prices — it builds a complete fueling strategy for every trip.

🔄

Cross-Company Comparison

Compare prices from IOCL, BPCL, HPCL, Nayara Energy, and Shell — all in one view. No more switching between apps.

📍

Route-Based Optimization

Not just the nearest pump — the cheapest fueling plan across your entire trip, accounting for detour time and fuel consumption.

🚗

Vehicle-Aware Calculations

Savings estimates personalized to your vehicle's actual mileage and tank capacity. Not generic averages.

📊

Tax Transparency

See exactly why prices differ — central excise, state VAT, dealer margin — all broken down clearly.

📅

Daily Price Updates

Fuel prices change every day at 6 AM. We pull fresh data from all oil marketing companies daily.

🚛

Fleet Management (Premium)

Multi-vehicle tracking, monthly fuel expense reports, and route history analytics for fleet operators.
